Why The App Exists?
People need a place to organize and share their favorite drink recipes. There are so many drinks that can be made and mixed, but remembering what the drinks are called, let alone how to make them can be difficult. What we want our app to be is a place that you can make and save your own drinks on your phone while also be able to explore what other people have made themselves and wanted to share with others.
